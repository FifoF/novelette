Novelette - Documentation

THIS HTML FILE IS A WORK IN PROGRESS. ANYTHING MAY CHANGE.

Document class novelette is unlike any other LaTeX document class: (a) It requires LuaLaTeX; (b) It does not honor widely-used LaTeX commands; (c) It has its own methods for setting layout and other properties, specifically written for those who have very little knowledge of LaTeX; (d) The resulting PDF is created for printed (to paper) popular fiction, such as detective novels, printed in black ink only; (e) There is no capability for Ebooks or any kind of academic works.

There are separate HTML pages for these topics:

  • The Preamble contains a small number of required lines, plus settings of your choice. Novelette is pre-configured for a common softcover print-on-demand format. Change as much or as little as you wish.
  • In front matter you will place essential information about your book: title, copyright, and a few other pages.
  • The main matter you place the story itself, and possibly material (such as maps) that are part of the story.
  • Novelette is not intended for academic works. There is no special provision for "backmatter" such as Appendix or Bibliography.
